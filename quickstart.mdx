<Steps>
  <Step title="Register an account with Rapid">
    Signing up takes less than a minute. Get started [here](https://rapidapi.com/auth/sign-up).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose the right plan for your Business">
    After signing in to Rapid, select the plan that meets your needs. The BASIC plan is free forever.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Test it out">
    Once subscribed to a plan, head over to the [playground](https://rapidapi.com/robince885/api/get-medium-data/playground/apiendpoint_1846f15b-3af8-4e1e-9332-df1e77faa53e) to test the endpoints. The playground includes sample code snippets in popular programming languages, making it easy to integrate into your projects.
  </Step>
</Steps>
